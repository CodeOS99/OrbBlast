# Orb Blast

Orb Blast is a Superhot clone in 2D.

## Controls
WASD to move
E to pickup
Left Click to shoot/toggle spike

## Guide
When you dont move, time moves at 1/10 the speed it generally does. When you move your mouse, it moves at half, and when you move, time moves at its regular speed..
**This includes enemy spawn and attack rates!**
So, when there are no enemies(including the beginning), or if things are getting a bit easy, feel free to move around a bit.

ENEMIES:

- Red: This enemy will move towards you. When close enough, it will turn yellow and "dash" towards your previous position, getting destroyed afterwards.

- Blue: This is a turret. It will move until its visible, then start shooting you between fixed intervals of time.

WEAPONS:

- Spike: Your default weapon, if you don't have a gun. Its super short range, and its difficult to hit with. Hold left mouse click to make it use it.

- Gun: Each destroyed enemy has a 50% chance to drop a gun. Each dropped gun refills your ammo to 2(it doesn't increase by 2, it refills it to 2). Move your cursor to aim and press left mouse buttong to shoot. .
